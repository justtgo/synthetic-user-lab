How do you use reviews when buying from a new brand?
Alex
First check is always the recent ones — like, last 2-3 months. If reviews are all from 2023, I assume the product or quality has changed and I get nervous. But here's the thing — I don't trust the brand's own review section anymore. Too many of those look astroturfed. So I'll usually go to Reddit and search "[brand name] review" to see what actual people say. If there's a r/femalefashionadvice thread, I read that. Trustpilot for shipping/returns issues specifically. Honestly it's a lot of work. Sometimes I just give up and stick with brands I know.
